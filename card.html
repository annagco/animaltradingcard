Chewie the Flerken

Flerkens hardly ever show mercy towards any living being. They are a deadly threat to both human and space creatures. When its mouth opens deadly tentacles come out, pull the prey and eat them in one bite.

  • Species: Flerken
  • Universe: Earth-616
  • Gender: Female
  • Threat Level: Extreme

Chewie is often mistaken for a normal earth cat, but she is far from being one. She is named after Chewbacca from Star Wars. She is brown and has yellow eyes.
